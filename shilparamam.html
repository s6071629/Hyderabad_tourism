Shilparamam Arts & Crafts Village, Jubilee Hills

About

An open cultural village showcasing India's traditional crafts, rural-village life, rock sculptures, and seasonal cultural performances—all set amid lush greenery in the IT-focused area of Jubilee Hills, Hyderabad.

  • Location: Jubilee Enclave, HITEC City (Madhapur), Hyderabad
  • Timings: 10:30 AM - 8:30 PM
  • Entry Fee: Adults ₹60, Children ₹20; optional: Boating ₹30, Battery-cart ₹15
