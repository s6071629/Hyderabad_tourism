Golconda Fort, Hyderabad

About

Historic fortress from the 12th century, famous for acoustics, palaces, and the evening light & sound show.

  • Location: Ibrahim Bagh, Hyderabad
  • Timings: 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM
  • Entry Fee: Indians ₹25, Foreigners ₹100; Light Show ₹140–₹200
