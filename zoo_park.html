Nehru Zoological Park, Hyderabad

About

Spread over 380 acres, this is one of India's largest zoos, home to tigers, lions, elephants, reptiles, and a safari park. Popular for families and kids.

  • Location: Bahadurpura, near Mir Alam Tank, Hyderabad
  • Timings: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Closed on Mondays)
  • Entry Fee: Adults ₹60, Children ₹40; Safari ₹50-₹100
