Durgam Chervu(Secret Lake), Hyderabad

About

Scenic lake surrounded by rocks, with a suspension bridge, cafés, and leisure activities.

  • Location: Madhapur, Hyderabad
  • Timings: 10:00 AM - 6:30 PM(Bridge view open till late night)
  • Entry Fee: Free (boating and activities extra ₹50-₹200)
