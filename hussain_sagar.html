Hussain Sagar Lake, Hyderabad

About

Man-made lake built in 1563, famous for the giant Buddha statue and boating. Surrounded by Tank Bund, Necklace Road, and lively parks.

  • Location: Between Hyderabad & Secunderabad
  • Timings: Daily, 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM
  • Entry Fee: Free (Boating charges ₹55–₹110)

Nearby Places

Lumbini Park (0.5 km)

Lakeside park with musical fountain and laser light shows.

Timings: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Entry Fee: ₹20 (Adults), ₹10 (Children)

Buddha Statue (in the lake)

Iconic 18m granite Buddha statue located on Gibraltar Rock in the lake, accessible by boat.

Timings: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Entry Fee: ₹55-₹110 (boating charges)

Necklace Road (2 km)

Scenic boulevard along the lake with eateries, gardens, and evening lights.

Timings: Open all day

Entry Fee: Free

NTR Gardens (2.5 km)

Urban park with toy train, rides, Japanese garden & fountains.

Timings: 2:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Entry Fee: ₹20 (Adults), ₹10 (Children)

Birla Mandir (3 km)

White marble temple offering panoramic views of Hyderabad.

Timings: 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM, 3:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Entry Fee: Free
