NTR Gardens, Hyderabad

About

A vibrant urban park near Hussain Sagar Lake featuring landscaped gardens, fountains, a Japanese garden, toy train, and fun rides. Great for evening relaxation and family outings.

  • Location: Hussain Sagar Lake, Hyderabad
  • Timings: 2:30 PM - 8:30 PM (Weekdays), 12:30 PM - 9:00 PM (Weekends)
  • Entry Fee: Adults ₹20, Children ₹10, Camera charges extra
