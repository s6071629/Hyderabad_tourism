Birla Mandir, Hyderabad

About

A serene white marble temple dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, offering panoramic views of Hyderabad.

  • Location: Naubat Pahad, Hyderabad
  • Timings: 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM, 3:00 PM - 9:00 PM
  • Entry Fee: Free

Nearby Places

Timings: 10:30 AM - 8:00 PM

Includes archaeology, interactive science displays, and a dinosaurium.

Timings: 10:30 AM - 8:00 PM

Entry Fee: ₹50 - ₹150 depending on sections

Lumbini Park (2.5 km)

Lakeside park with musical fountains and laser light shows near Hussain Sagar.

Timings: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM

Entry Fee: ₹20 (Adults), ₹10 (Children)

Hussain Sagar Lake(3 km)

Iconic heart-shaped lake with the giant Buddha statue in the middle. Famous for boating and evening lights.

Timings: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Entry Fee: Free (boating charges extra: ₹55 - ₹110 approx.)

Necklace Road (3 km)

Lakeside promenade with eateries, gardens, and great views of the lake at night.

Timings: Open all day

Entry Fee: Free

NTR Gardens (3 km)

A lush urban park with fun rides, toy train, Japanese garden, and fountains.

Timings: 2:30 PM - 8:30 PM (Weekdays), 12:30 PM - 9:00 PM (Weekends)

Entry Fee: ₹20 (Adults), ₹10 (Children)
