Ramoji Film City, Hyderabad

About

One of the world's largest film studio complexes, spread over 2000 acres. A blend of movie sets, theme parks, gardens, adventure zones, and live shows—ideal for both cinema lovers and family fun.

  • Location: Abdullapurmet, Hyderabad (approx. 30 km from city centre)
  • Timings: Daily, 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM
  • Entry Fee:
    • General Day Tour: Adults ₹1,150, Children (3-12 yrs) ₹950
    • Special Packages: ₹2,350+ (includes rides, shows & meals)
