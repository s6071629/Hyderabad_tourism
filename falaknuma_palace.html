Falaknuma Palace, Hyderabad

About

A grand 19th-century palace built by the Nizam, known as the “Mirror of the Sky.” Now a luxury Taj hotel, famous for its Venetian chandeliers, massive dining hall, and stunning hilltop views.

  • Location: Engine Bowli, Falaknuma, Hyderabad
  • Timings: Guided tours (by prior booking) usually 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM (Closed Monday)
  • Entry Fee: Around ₹3000-₹4000 per person (tour + hi-tea package, via Taj)
