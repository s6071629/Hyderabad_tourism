Lumbini Park, Hyderabad

About

Lakeside park near Hussain Sagar, famous for laser shows, musical fountains, and boating to the Buddha statue.

  • Location: Tank Bund Road, Hyderabad
  • Timings: 9:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Entry Fee: ₹20 (Adults), ₹10 (Children), Laser Show ₹50

Nearby Places

Hussain Sagar Lake (0.5 km)

Iconic lake with the Buddha statue and boating activities.

Timings: 8:00 AM - 10:00 PM

Entry Fee: Free (boating ₹55-₹110)

Buddha Statue (via boat)

18m granite Buddha statue on Gibraltar Rock, accessible by boat.

Timings: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Entry Fee: Boating ₹55-₹110

NTR Gardens (1 km)

Urban park with toy train, gardens, and fountains.

Timings: 2:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Entry Fee: ₹20 (Adults), ₹10 (Children)

Necklace Road (1.5 km)

Birla Mandir (3 km)

White marble temple with panoramic views of Hyderabad.

Timings: 7:00 AM - 12:00 PM, 3:00 PM - 9:00 PM
