Chowmahalla Palace, Hyderabad

About

Former seat of the Nizams, famous for its stunning courtyards, durbar hall, and royal collections.

  • Location: Khilwat, near Charminar, Hyderabad
  • Timings: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Closed on Fridays)
  • Entry Fee: ₹80 (Indians), ₹200 (Foreigners), ₹10 (Children)
