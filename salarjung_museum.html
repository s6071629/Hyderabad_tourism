Salar Jung Museum, Hyderabad

About

One of India's largest museums, housing rare art, antiques, manuscripts, sculptures, and artifacts from across the world. Famous pieces include the Veiled Rebecca and double-figure wooden clock.

  • Location: Darul Shifa, on the southern bank of Musi River, Hyderabad
  • Timings: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Closed Mondays & Govt. Holidays)
  • Entry Fee: Indians ₹50, Foreigners ₹500, Students & Kids (under 18) Free
