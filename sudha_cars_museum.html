Sudha Cars Museum, Hyderabad

About

A quirky museum by K. Sudhakar showcasing handmade cars shaped like shoes, handbags, pens, cycles, and more.

  • Location: Bahadurpura, near Zoo Park, Hyderabad
  • Timings: 9:30 AM - 6:30 PM
  • Entry Fee: Adults ₹50-150, Kids ₹15-100, Camera ₹100
